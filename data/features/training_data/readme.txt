Data generated for training the neural network from the corpus/training/ directory.

Files:

in1.txt and out1.txt - rows corresponding to the feature vectors generated from different authors and their targets (randomly)

diff_pairs.txt - names of author/work pairs corresponding to feature vectors inside in1.txt

in2.txt and out2.txt - rows corresponding to the feature vectors generated from same authors with different works and their targets (randomly)

same_pairs.txt - names of author/work pairs corresponding to feature vectors inside of in2.txt
