Data generated for training the neural network from the corpus/ingroup/ directory.

Files:

all_diff_pairs.txt and out1.txt - rows corresponding to the feature vectors generated from different authors and their targets (randomly)

diff_pair_names.txt - names of author/work pairs corresponding to feature vectors inside all_diff_pairs.txt

all_same_pairs.txt and out2.txt - rows corresponding to the feature vectors generated from same authors with different works and their targets (randomly)

same_pair_names.txt - names of author/work pairs corresponding to feature vectors inside of all_same_pairs.txt
